## History

Founded in **1994** by a group of engineers who built their careers at Zambia Consolidated Copper Mines (ZCCM). The firm was established to serve newly privatized mining operations on the Zambian and DRC Copperbelt following the ZCCM breakup. Scope has since expanded across sub-Saharan Africa.

## Services

### 1. Mining Services
Resource and reserve estimation and audits, open pit and underground optimisation, mine production scheduling, feasibility studies, stockpile/dump volume surveys, mining software training, on-site mine planning support.

### 2. Exploration & Geological Services
Geological mapping, geochemical soil sampling, ground geophysics, drill programme management, drill core processing, statutory government reporting and compliance.

### 3. GIS & Environmental Services
Environmental baseline research, Environmental Project Briefs (EPBs), impact assessments, audits, environmental management plans, drone mapping and surveying.

### 4. Metallurgical Services
Process design and plant optimisation, flotation reagents and flocculants supply (via Kemira SA agency agreement), after-sale technical support. Senior Metallurgical Consultant: Dr. Yotam Hara.

### 5. Geotechnical Engineering Services
Waste dump and tailings dam design, slope stability assessment, numerical modelling, rock mass classification and characterisation, site investigations for mining and infrastructure projects.

### 6. Strategy & Investment Advisory
Market entry support, financial modelling and valuations, financial structuring, organisational improvement, ESG advisory.
