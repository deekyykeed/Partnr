01 — Mining Services

Mine planning and optimisation from first principles

We provide rigorous technical support across the full mining value chain — from initial resource estimation through to on-site production planning. Our work is grounded in real Copperbelt experience, combining practical knowledge with industry-standard software and methodology.

  • Resource and reserve estimation and independent audits
  • Open pit and underground mine design and optimisation
  • Mine production scheduling and planning
  • Feasibility studies (scoping, pre-feasibility, bankable)
  • Stockpile and waste dump volume surveys
  • Mining software training (Surpac, Whittle, Vulcan)
  • On-site mine planning support and secondments

Finding what's in the ground — with precision

03 — GIS & Environmental Services

Environmental compliance built on rigorous science

Environmental permitting and compliance are non-negotiable for modern mining operations. We provide technically defensible environmental studies, assessments, and management plans — built on thorough baseline data and designed to satisfy both regulators and financiers.

  • Environmental baseline studies and data collection
  • Environmental Project Briefs (EPBs)
  • Environmental and Social Impact Assessments (ESIAs)
  • Environmental audits and compliance reviews
  • Environmental Management Plans (EMPs)
  • Drone mapping, aerial surveying, and volumetric analysis

05 — Geotechnical Engineering Services

Ground engineering for safe, long-term mine operations

Geotechnical failure is one of the most consequential risks in mining. Our team provides rigorous slope stability analysis, waste facility design, and ground characterisation — giving operators the confidence that their infrastructure will perform safely over the life of mine.

  • Waste dump and tailings dam design
  • Tailings storage facility (TSF) stability reviews
  • Slope stability assessment and monitoring programmes
  • Numerical modelling (finite element / finite difference)
  • Rock mass classification and characterisation
  • Site investigations for mining and civil infrastructure
  • Geotechnical risk assessment and management plans
