Our Story

Built from the inside out

AMC Africa was established in 1994 by a group of engineers who had spent their careers at Zambia Consolidated Copper Mines (ZCCM) — the state enterprise that operated the entire Zambian Copperbelt for decades. When ZCCM was privatised, these engineers founded AMC to serve the newly fragmented mining operations with the kind of institutional knowledge that cannot be learned from a textbook.

That founding insight — that the best mining advice comes from people who have actually worked the ground — has shaped everything about how we operate. We employ over 19 staff with 30+ specialist associates, all with deep sub-Saharan African experience. Our work spans Zambia, the DRC, and increasingly across the continent.

Our key differentiator is not simply that we are local. It is that we combine genuine local experience with the systems, standards, and rigour of international engineering practice. That combination is what Glencore, Anglo American, and First Quantum Minerals rely on us for.
