About AMC Africa

Thirty years of expertise.
Built on the Copperbelt.

African Mining Consultants was founded in 1994 by engineers who built their careers inside ZCCM — the state corporation that shaped Zambia's copper industry. We have never left the continent. That is our advantage.

Our founding

From inside the
industry

AMC Africa was established by ex-ZCCM engineers who understood that the gap in the market was not technical expertise — it was local expertise. International firms had the credentials. Nobody had the ground-level knowledge of Zambian geology, regulation, and operating conditions that only decades of in-country work could build.

What we do

Six practice areas.
One integrated team.

01

Mining Services

Feasibility studies, mine planning, resource estimation, and production optimisation across open-pit and underground operations.

02

Exploration & Geological Services

Regional and project-scale exploration targeting, geological mapping, drill programme design, and resource definition.

03

GIS & Environmental Services

Environmental impact assessments, baseline studies, monitoring programmes, and spatial data management.

04

Metallurgical Services

Mineralogical characterisation, process flowsheet development, pilot test work, and plant performance reviews.

05

Geotechnical Engineering

Slope stability analysis, tailings dam assessments, ground support design, and geomechanical modelling.

06

Strategy & Investment Advisory

Bankable feasibility studies, technical due diligence, transaction support, and mining sector market entry advisory.
