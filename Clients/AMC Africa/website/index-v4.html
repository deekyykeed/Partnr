What We Do

Six Disciplines.
One Firm.

From feasibility to full delivery — we cover the complete technical spectrum of African mining and exploration.

01
Mining Services
Open-cast and underground mining studies, mine planning, scheduling, and production optimisation for base metals and gold operations across sub-Saharan Africa.
02
Exploration & Geological Services
Resource estimation, geological mapping, drill programme design, and reporting to JORC or SAMREC standards. Trusted by juniors and majors alike.
03
GIS & Environmental Services
Environmental impact assessments, regulatory compliance, land-use mapping, and GIS data management across multi-jurisdiction projects.
04
Metallurgical Services
Process plant design, metallurgical testwork, flow-sheet development, and optimisation of copper, cobalt, and gold recovery circuits.
05
Geotechnical Engineering
Pit slope stability, underground rock mechanics, tailings facility design, and infrastructure geotechnical investigations.
06
Strategy & Investment Advisory
Bankable feasibility studies, due diligence, valuation, and strategic advisory for mining investors, lenders, and government bodies.

01 — Origin
Built on the Copperbelt

Founded in 1994 by engineers who built their careers at ZCCM — Zambia Consolidated Copper Mines. That institutional knowledge of African ore bodies, regulators, and logistics is not taught in textbooks. It takes decades in the field.

02 — Capability
International-Grade Delivery

AMC's local expertise is supported by relationships with several international engineering groups. Clients receive Copperbelt know-how combined with global technical standards — JORC, SAMREC, IFC Performance Standards.

03 — Track Record
Trusted Where It Counts

Glencore, Anglo American, and First Quantum Minerals have each engaged AMC for critical project work. For the world's largest mining groups, reputation is everything — ours has been earned contract by contract, over thirty years.
