— Questions

Frequently Asked

What industries do you serve?
We primarily serve the mining and minerals sector — base metals (copper, cobalt, zinc), gold, and related industries. We also support energy infrastructure projects including solar and hydropower, where our geotechnical and environmental expertise directly applies.
Do you work across Africa or only in Zambia?
We are based in Kitwe, Zambia, with a second office in Lubumbashi, DRC. We have delivered projects across multiple countries in sub-Saharan Africa and continue to expand wherever the project demands.
Can you produce bankable feasibility studies?
Yes. We have delivered bankable-standard feasibility studies, prefeasibility studies, and JORC-compliant resource and reserve estimates for projects financed by international institutions. Our work has been signed off by independent technical experts and submitted to regulatory bodies across Zambia and the DRC.
Do you work with junior miners or only the majors?
We work across the full spectrum. While our track record includes world-class clients like Glencore and Anglo American, we also partner with junior explorers, emerging producers, and development-stage projects. The rigour we apply is the same regardless of company size.
What is the typical engagement process?
We begin with a consultation to understand your project scope, timeline, and technical requirements. We then propose a defined scope of work with clear deliverables and pricing. Most engagements move from consultation to proposal within one to two weeks.
