What We Do

Six disciplines, one integrated team.

From exploration to environmental compliance — we cover the full spectrum of technical consulting that African mining operations demand.

01 Mining Services Feasibility studies, mine planning, production optimisation, and technical due diligence for operations of all scales.
02 Exploration & Geology Resource estimation, drill programme management, and geological mapping across the Copperbelt and beyond.
03 GIS & Environment Environmental impact assessments, spatial data analysis, and compliance monitoring for regulatory requirements.
04 Metallurgical Services Process design, metallurgical testwork, and plant optimisation to maximise recovery and reduce costs.
05 Geotechnical Engineering Slope stability analysis, ground control, and geotechnical risk assessment for open-pit and underground operations.
06 Strategy & Investment Market entry advisory, investment appraisal, and commercial strategy for mining companies and project developers.
