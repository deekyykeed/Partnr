# Background Replacement Prompt

Swap `[COLOR]` for any color name or hex value. Works for people and objects.

---

```
Photorealistic background replacement only. The subject must be preserved exactly as photographed — do not alter, enhance, retouch, or modify the subject in any way.

If the subject is a person: preserve all skin tone, skin texture, facial features, hair color, hair texture, hair styling, clothing, fabric texture, clothing color, fit, accessories, jewelry, and all styling details.

If the subject is an object or vehicle: preserve all surface finish, color, texture, material, reflections, wear, branding, decals, mechanical details, and any attached components exactly as they appear.

Replace only the background with a solid flat [COLOR] background. Single uniform color — no gradients, no patterns, no texture.

Add a soft, diffused shadow cast onto the background behind the subject — as if the subject is standing approximately one to two feet in front of a solid [COLOR] backdrop with natural studio lighting. The shadow should fall softly on the background wall, not on the ground, giving the subject a sense of depth and separation from the background. The shadow must be subtle, with soft edges and natural falloff — not a hard outline.

The edge between subject and background must be seamless — preserve fine details like hair strands, fabric edges, transparent elements, or mechanical protrusions. No halo, no fringing, no cutout appearance.

Final result should look like a professional studio photograph shot against a [COLOR] backdrop, ready for direct placement on a website.
```
