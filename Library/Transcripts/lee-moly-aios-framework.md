## Key Takeaways

- AIOS is not a business model — it's a methodology. A wrapper around your existing business, built in layers not leaps.
- The operator trap: most founders spend 80% of time IN the business (firefighting) and 20% ON it (growing). AIOS flips this.
- The 5 layers: Context → Data → Intelligence → Automate → Build. Each layer removes more overhead.
- The daily brief is one of the most powerful early wins — a morning PDF/Telegram summary of everything that happened (meetings, Slack, data, key signals).
- Away-from-desk autonomy is a real test — can you run your business from your phone? That's the north star.
- Revenue per employee is the new flex metric in the AI era — not headcount, efficiency.
- Skills (documented workflows) are shareable and installable — this is what makes the system packageable and sellable.
- Small businesses have a structural advantage over large ones here — nimble, no ERP drag, can freely connect APIs.
- Lee generated $1M NZD in 7 days by pouring freed-up bandwidth into a new webinar campaign.

---

## The 5 Build Layers

| Layer | What it is |
|-------|-----------|
| **1. Context** | Business knowledge fed into the AI — who you are, what you sell, team, values, strategy |
| **2. Data** | All data sources connected and queryable — revenue, CRM, Stripe, analytics |
| **3. Intelligence** | Meetings (Fireflies) + comms (Slack) logged → powers the daily brief |
| **4. Automate** | Task audit → identify what to remove or augment → build automations (cron jobs) |
| **5. Build** | Use freed-up bandwidth to launch new initiatives that grow the business |

---

## The 3 KPIs

| KPI | What it measures |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Away-from-desk autonomy** | Can you run your business 100% from your phone when away? |
| **Task automation %** | What % of your task audit have you automated or heavily augmented? (Target: 60–70%) |
| **Revenue per employee** | Efficiency metric — revenue up, headcount flat or shrinks |

## The Operator Trap (Bandwidth Diagram)

```
BEFORE AIOS:
[========================================] 80% IN the business (admin, ops, firefighting)
[========] 20% ON the business (growth, new products, new channels)

AFTER AIOS:
[========] 20% IN the business
[========================================] 80% ON the business
```

Each layer you add moves the line a little further.
