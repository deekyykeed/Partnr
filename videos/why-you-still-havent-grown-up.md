# Why You Still Haven't Grown Up

**Status:** In Progress
**Topic:** Puer Eternus — the Jungian concept of the eternal child
**Source:** Lecture transcript by Dr. K (adapted)

---

## Summary

A deep dive into Carl Jung's concept of Puer Eternus — the archetype of someone who remains an eternal child. Covers why intelligent, high-potential people fail to launch, the psychological cycle that keeps them stuck, how modern technology amplifies it, and the one thing that actually breaks the pattern: being willing to be ordinary.

---

## Core Thesis

The reason gifted, high-potential people stay stuck is not lack of intelligence or talent. It is a pathological inability to make commitments — rooted in the fear of closing off possibilities. The cure is not a hack or a secret technique. It is choosing to be trapped, ordinary, and drab — on purpose.

---

## Key Concepts

- **Puer Eternus / Puella Eterna** — Jung's archetype of the eternal child
- **Pluripotent stem cell** — can become anything, which is why it commits to nothing
- **Marginal / provisional life** — living in the boarding area, never boarding the plane
- **Failure to constellate** — individual stars that never form a picture
- **The hair in the soup** — one flaw invalidates the whole bowl
- **The heroic workaround** — doing extraordinary things to avoid ordinary ones
