# Slides — Part 2: The Characteristics

**SLIDE 1**
# Why You Still Haven't Grown Up
### Part 2: What Does the Puer Actually Look Like?

---

**SLIDE 2**
## First Impression: Impressive
- Charming, charismatic, likable
- Often the funniest person in the group
- Quick-witted, intelligent
- People meet them and think: *this person is going places*

---

**SLIDE 3**
## Second Impression: Frustrating
- Starts strong — falls flat
- Can't follow through consistently
- Ability to deliver on expectations: falls absolutely flat
- Potential is real. Goes nowhere.

---

**SLIDE 4**
## The Sleepy Haze
- Appears lazy, unfocused, drifting
- *"The sleepy, undisciplined youth who merely hangs around"*
- Makes you want to pour a bucket of cold water over their head
- But underneath: a rich, vivid inner fantasy life

---

**SLIDE 5**
## What They're Actually Like Inside
- Hate small talk — love big ideas
- Want to understand quantum mechanics, not make small talk at a water cooler
- Deep thinkers, big dreamers
- The inner world is alive. The outer world is the problem.

---

**SLIDE 6**
## The Pattern
- Big talkers
- Don't show up when it counts
- Everyone sees the potential
- Nobody — including them — can figure out why it never lands
