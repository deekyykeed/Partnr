# Slides — Part 3: Fantasy vs. Reality

**SLIDE 1**
# Why You Still Haven't Grown Up
### Part 3: Why Reality Always Disappoints

---

**SLIDE 2**
## The Core Problem
- Big dreams. Big ideas. Enormous intelligence.
- But every time they engage with real life —
- Reality disappoints.

---

**SLIDE 3**
## The Gap
- Fantasy: vivid, exciting, perfect
- Reality: drab, grinding, flawed
- The imagined version is always better than the lived version

---

**SLIDE 4**
## This Happens Everywhere
- The novel they want to write
- The video game they want to build
- The career they envision
- *The moment they start — it's already a shadow of the dream*

---

**SLIDE 5**
## It Happens in Relationships Too
- Searching for the perfect version of a person
- Each time fascinated → discovers they're just a human being → disappointed → moves on
- *It's not women on a pedestal. It's life on a pedestal.*

---

**SLIDE 6**
## The Cycle
- Vision → Engage → Disappointment → Retreat
- Back to the fantasy
- Reset. Repeat.
- *The fantasy is always safer than the reality*
