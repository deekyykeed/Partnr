**SLIDE 1**
# Why You Still Haven't Grown Up
### Part 5: The Cycle & The Defenses

---

**SLIDE 2**
## What the Cycle Looks Like
- Excitement → start → obstacle or boredom → retreat
- Back to fantasy: games, social media, escape
- New itch → new project → same result
- *10 steps in 15 directions*

---

**SLIDE 3**
## Even When Life Forces a Choice
- Reluctantly get a job — hate it, it's beneath them
- Half-ass it — it's not the dream
- Long for the fantasy even more
- Still won't commit — but also won't quit
- *Perpetual loading zone*

---

**SLIDE 4**
## Defense #1: Circumstance Hunting
- "In the perfect circumstances, my greatness will emerge"
- Constantly searching for the right job / city / people / moment
- *"I know I can do it — I just need the right conditions"*
- The conditions never arrive

---

**SLIDE 5**
## Defense #2: Blame
- Blame others. Blame circumstances.
- Sneakiest version: **blame past self**
- *"If I'd made the right choice back then, I wouldn't be here"*
- Sounds like responsibility. Isn't.
- They don't change now — which reveals it's not accountability

---

**SLIDE 6**
## Defense #3: Fragility
- Health, mental health, trauma — used as permanent shields
- These can be genuinely real and hard
- But the Puer uses them to avoid the drab work
- *"I would rather believe it's unsolvable than do the boring steps to solve it"*

---

**SLIDE 7**
## The Cruel Irony
- The thing they fear most: wasting their life
- The cycle they're in: wastes their life
- Every year in the loading zone is the outcome they were trying to avoid
