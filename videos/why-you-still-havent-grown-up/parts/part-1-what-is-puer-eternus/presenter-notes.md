# Presenter Notes — Part 1: What Is Puer Eternus?

---

**SLIDE 1 — Title**
- Open with the big claim: smart, high-potential people stay stuck — and it's not about IQ or resources
- This is a series — set up that each video covers one concept
- Don't over-explain. Drop the hook and move.

---

**SLIDE 2 — The Root of the Problem**
- There are a thousand explanations out there: dopamine, executive function, trauma, whatever
- Jung cut through all of it and said: no. There's one thing.
- That's why we're going through his framework — because it actually gets to the root
- *"It has everything to do with one psychological pattern."*

---

**SLIDE 3 — Puer Eternus**
- Define it clean: Puer = boy, Puella = girl, Eternus = eternal
- Applies to men and women — colloquially called Puer but it's gender-neutral
- Over 100 years ago, Jung described this archetype
- This is not about being immature or childish in a fun way — it's a specific psychological block

---

**SLIDE 4 — Who Does This Affect?**
- Speak to the audience: these are often the smartest people in the room
- Do well in school, something happens around university / early adulthood — fail to launch
- Key line: *"Gifted kids are special needs kids"* — let that land, it's counterintuitive
- Gamers, creatives, people with obvious potential who never seem to go anywhere

---

**SLIDE 5 — Why Jung?**
- Everyone else has a different explanation — neuroscience has a thousand of them
- Jung said: strip it back, here's the one thing underneath all of it
- Von Franz (his colleague) wrote a whole book on it — published in the 1950s
- Stress the eerie accuracy: a concept from 70 years ago that perfectly describes what's happening today

---

**SLIDE 6 — Technology Makes It Worse**
- This archetype has always existed — but it was a minority
- Technology is the *critical hit* — it activates and amplifies the vulnerability perfectly
- That's why we're seeing developmental stunting at a generational scale
- Close the intro: *"That's what this series is about. Let's start at the beginning."*
- Tease Part 2 briefly: next video covers what the Puer actually looks like
