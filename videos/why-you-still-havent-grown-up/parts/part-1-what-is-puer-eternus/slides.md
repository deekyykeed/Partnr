**SLIDE 2**
## The Root of the Problem
- Not IQ. Not resources. Not circumstances.
- One psychological block — identified 100 years ago
- Jung called it: **Puer Eternus**

---

**SLIDE 3**
## Puer Eternus
*The Eternal Child*
- Puer = boy / Puella = girl
- Eternus = eternal
- The archetype of someone who never grows up

---

**SLIDE 4**
## Who Does This Affect?
- Gifted kids who fail to launch
- High-IQ people stuck in gaming
- High-potential people who can't follow through
- *"Gifted kids are special needs kids"*

---

**SLIDE 5**
## Why Jung?
- A thousand theories exist: dopamine, frontal lobe, trauma
- Jung said: *no — this is it*
- He found the single psychological root
- Von Franz wrote the book — conceived in the 1950s, eerie how relevant it is today

---

**SLIDE 6**
## Technology Makes It Worse
- This archetype has always existed
- Modern tech is the *critical hit* for this vulnerability
- We are seeing mass developmental stunting
- This is why it matters right now
