## The Hair in the Soup
- A whole bowl of delicious soup
- One hair → it's ruined → can't eat it
- Can't take the hair out. Can't be grateful for the soup.
- *There is always a hair*

**SLIDE 3**
## The Rucksack
- Mountaineer who hated carrying a bag
- Trained yogic breathing to sleep in snow
- Went without food to avoid carrying weight
- Heroic, absurd effort — to avoid the ordinary
- *"You can just carry the bag, bro"*

---

**SLIDE 4**
## Exceptional or Nothing
- Will do extraordinary things to avoid ordinary ones
- Cannot be a normie — would rather suffer heroically
- *"I will be exceptional or I will be nothing"*
- The only option is extraordinary

---

**SLIDE 5**
## Technology Is the Perfect Trap
- Video games: be exceptional without real-world cost
- Pornography: the fantasy relationship, on demand
- Social media: performance without commitment
- Puer Eternus used to be a fraction of the population
- *It is becoming the majority*

---

**SLIDE 6**
## The Red Pill Irony
- Red pill = making the hard choice, seeing reality
- But idealising it is the fantasy — it's actually the blue pill
- *"The people who talk about the red pill are the most bluepilled"*
- Living a hero fantasy instead of doing ordinary, unglamorous work

---

**SLIDE 7**
## This Applies to Everyone
- Puella Eterna: the fantasy of motherhood without the work
- Biohackers: optimise everything to avoid just eating well and exercising
- Guru culture: selling extraordinary shortcuts to people who won't take ordinary steps
- *Why does this industry exist? Because we are a society of Puera Eterni*
