## What They Do

AMC is an exploration, mining, geotechnical and environmental engineering consultancy. Their tagline: **"Africa's Exploration, Mining & Environmental Engineering Experts."**

### Services

1. **Mining Services** — Open pit & underground mine design, resource/reserve estimation, production scheduling, feasibility studies, mining software training, stockpile surveys, on-site planning.

2. **Exploration & Geological Services** — Geological mapping, geochemical soil sampling, ground geophysics, drilling program management, core cutting/sampling, statutory government reporting.

3. **GIS & Environmental Services** — Environmental baseline assessments, ESIAs (Environmental & Social Impact Assessments), environmental auditing, management plans, compliance reporting, drone mapping & surveying.

4. **Metallurgical Services** — Process design, supply of flotation reagents & flocculants, technical support, plant optimization. (Recent expansion via agency agreement with Kemira South Africa + Dr. Yotam Hara as Senior Metallurgical Consultant.)

5. **Geotechnical Engineering Services** — Waste dump & tailings dam design/audits, rock mass evaluation, numerical modelling, slope stability, site investigations, foundation assessments.

6. **Strategy & Investment Advisory** — Market entry, financial modelling, valuations, investment structuring, organisational enhancement, ESG advisory.
