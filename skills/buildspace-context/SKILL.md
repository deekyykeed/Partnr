# BuildSpace Context

## The Overarching Goal

Everything produced in this workspace serves one objective: **get more people into BuildSpace.**

This applies regardless of what the immediate task is. A video script, a slide deck, a quotation, a post — they're all working toward the same thing. Keep this in mind when making decisions about tone, framing, calls to action, and what to emphasise.

## How to Apply This

- **Videos and scripts**: Frame content so it demonstrates value and draws people toward BuildSpace. The speaker is an authority worth following. Every video is also a funnel.
- **Presentations**: Designed to be compelling and shareable. Leave the audience wanting to know more and knowing where to go.
- **Audience building**: Two-way relationships, authenticity, long-form content. The goal is to make BuildSpace a fixture in people's lives, not a one-off touchpoint.

## Tone Across All Work

Confident, direct, no fluff. Content should feel like it comes from someone who has done the work and is bringing people along — not selling at them.
