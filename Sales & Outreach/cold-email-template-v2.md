## How to personalise (fill in before sending)

| Placeholder | What to put |
|-------------|------------|
| `[First Name]` | Owner/director's first name if known, otherwise skip and use "Hi there" |
| `[law firms / engineering firms / etc.]` | Match to the company's category |
| `[Lusaka / Kitwe]` | Their city |
| `[Company Name]` | Their business name |

**Quick personalisation tip:** If you can find one specific detail about them (a recent award, a recognisable client name on their site, a specific service they highlight), drop it into the first sentence. E.g. *"Noticed you've been doing work for Zambia Revenue Authority — impressive."*
