## Notes for New Build

- AMC's site is dark-primary — `#0A0D14` base, light text
- Royal Blue `#0F4EB2` is the signature accent — buttons, highlights, links
- Teal `#44847C` and gold `#957340` are available as supporting accents
- Warm tones (tan, peru) suggest earthy/mining heritage — use sparingly for warmth
- White `#FFFFFF` for headings and high-contrast elements on dark backgrounds
