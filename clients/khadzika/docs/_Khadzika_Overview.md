## What They Do

Authorized HYTORC distributor for Zambia. Supply, hire, and repair hydraulic torque wrenches and industrial bolting equipment. Also deliver contracted industrial services (vegetation control, corrosion protection, tiling works) to major operators.

**Primary clients:** BIA Group, Mopani Copper Mines, KCM (Konkola Copper Mines), CEC (Copperbelt Energy Corporation), Zambia Sugar, FQM Trident, Dangote Cement, ZESCO

**Industries:** Mining (copper), energy, cement, agro-industrial
